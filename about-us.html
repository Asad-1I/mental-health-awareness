Who We Are

We are a mental health awareness organisation dedicated to improving the health and wellbeing of our users. Our team, which consists of two mental health professionals and a revolving door of volunteers, offers a range support including mental health coping strategies, nutritional advice, recommendations of mental health wellness retreats. All of which are available by our Contact Us link in the navigation bar.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality mental health well-being services and advice that improve the overall health and wellbeing of our users. Our team ethos is to empower indviduals through the means of mental health awareness. With the ultimate goal to provide our users with the resources they need to live healthier, happier and more fulfilling lives.

Our Team

John Doe

John is our mental health lead. John has been a mental health professional for the past 15 years. John has had experience in both the public and private sector as professional mental health worker. John now feels he wants to give back to people in non-profit program and believes reaching out via this website will help achieve this goal with the help of the team including Emily, the team of volunteers (one of whom help create the website) and the website user community.

Emily Johnson

Emily is our physical and nutritional health lead. Emily is a qualified yoga instructor and does so as a profession. Emily was introduced to Mindful of Mental Health by John who was and still is a student of Emilys yoga classes. John after a class mentioned the project Mindful of Mental Health to Emily. The project sounded very exciting to Emily who then committed to being the physical and nutrition lead of the project.
